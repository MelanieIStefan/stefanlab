NCS Grant Writing Group

This is a monthly data-club style meeting where we discuss our plans for upcoming grant applications and get feedback from a friendly, non-threatening group of peers. It can also be used to share insights from past writing successes or failures. Although it grew from the Neural Control Systems group within the Centre for Integrative Physiology, it is of course open to everyone interested. Drop me an e-mail if you would like to speak at one of our upcoming sessions: mstefan _at_ exseed.ed.ac.uk We meet every fourth Monday, ususally right after the Neural Control Systems meeting in Room 174.
